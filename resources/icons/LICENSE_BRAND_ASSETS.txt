Proprietary LibreSlicer brand artwork
-------------------------------------
Files:
  libreslicer_brand_wordmark.png
  libreslicer_brand_mark.png

These bitmap assets are proprietary to Mythic Reliquary LLC. They are
included in this repository so official builds can embed the LibreSlicer
wordmark and mark when `-DOFFICIAL_BRAND=ON` is used. Redistribution of
these specific images is permitted only as part of unmodified LibreSlicer
binaries produced from this repository. No trademark rights are granted
for forks or downstream projects.

Generic/community builds should rely on the CC0 placeholder artwork
(libreslicer_logo_wordmark.svg, libreslicer_logo_mark.svg) by leaving
`OFFICIAL_BRAND` at its default OFF value.
